What is Cypecad?

CYPECAD course, Cypecad is a structural design and calculation software from the company Cype Ingenieros, the program allows the design and calculation of reinforced concrete structures.

Additionally, complement the design of metal structures with the Cype 3d suite and calculation of water supply installations, electrical installations and thermal analysis of a building with Cypecad Mep.

CYPECAD Software, in addition to the structural design, also allows obtaining plans and lists of works, analysis of thermal loads, earthquake resistant design, wind design, integrated 3D visualization , among other faculties.
